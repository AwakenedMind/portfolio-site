DestroyGames
Feb 2019 - Current
DestroyGames is a gaming platform for playing .io games (popular multiplayer browser games). Players can easily visit the site and find their next favorite game because we collect the most popular games online into one big list.
Tech Used:
  • ReactJS
  • GatsbyJS
  • Styled Components
  • Contentful CMS
  • Firebase
  • FaunaDB
  • Netlify Auth
  • Figma
  • Jest
  • CircleCI
